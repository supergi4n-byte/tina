Service Overview

Our application provides a production-ready workflow to generate, review, and publish AI clips. Teams can manage assets, merge videos, and publish approved clips to TikTok with official API flows.

Operational Modules

Prompt Enhancement

Text prompts are refined to improve media output quality and visual coherence.

Video Generation

Videos are generated through configured providers and saved for review and processing.

Video Merge and Processing

Multiple clips can be merged with FFmpeg support before publication.

TikTok Publish Flow

Authorized users upload approved clips and publish via official TikTok APIs.

Scope and Permission Principle

We request only permissions needed to authenticate users and publish approved content. We remove any unused products or scopes from the developer configuration.
