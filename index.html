Official Website

This website provides complete information about our web services, platform usage, legal terms, and privacy commitments. Our Python-based platform helps teams create AI video clips, review content quality, and publish approved clips to TikTok through official developer integrations.

The end-user experience includes clear calls to action that direct viewers to our AI FAQ and order support page, so customers can quickly access help and order management tools.

What We Do

AI Video Workflow

We generate and process short clips from prompts and media assets. Internal operators review each clip before publication.

TikTok Publishing

Authorized team members publish approved videos using TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API, with role-based operational controls.

Customer Guidance

Published content can direct users to support pages for AI-powered FAQ and order handling, reducing friction in post-purchase support.

Compliance First

We request only the scopes our app needs, use official APIs, and maintain audit-friendly publishing records for quality and policy compliance.

TikTok Developer Scope Statement

We request only minimum required permissions to authenticate users and publish approved video content. We do not request unnecessary products or scopes. Scope details and use cases are documented for app review.

  • Authentication scope: connect and authorize the correct TikTok account.
  • Posting scope: upload and publish approved clips.
  • No extra scope requests beyond operational necessity.
